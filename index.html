Fang Kong

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in John Hopcroft Center, Shanghai Jiao Tong University under the supervision of Prof. Shuai Li. I am also a member in Wu Honor Ph.D. Class in Artificial Intelligence, Shanghai Jiao Tong University starting from Autumn 2020. Before joining SJTU, I obtained my bachelor degree in Software engineering from Shandong University (1/318).

I'm interested in bandit theory and reinforcement learning theory.
